#Keep the Color

JC Booth’s SLED team brainstormed a fundraiser called “Keep The Color”. The idea was to decorate the walls of JC Booth more creatively . First, we planned together how we would organize this event. Then, we sought advice from the art teacher, Ms. Raymer. After, we edited our plan and presented to district leaders. We were ready to implement the fundraiser. Due to the many challenges we have including digital learning, we are passing this project on to the next SLED team.
