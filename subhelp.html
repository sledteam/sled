SUB Help

The members in our SLED team noticed that subs in the morning were often confused about which classroom to go to, how to log in to the Smartboard, how to access the announcements, and more. So, our team came together and started to brainstorm ideas of what we could do to help. While brainstorming, we realized that we can also include distributing the booth bucks into Subhelp. We then started to design a bucket to store the boothbucks, folders, and and logs. We figured out that we would help subs every morning with their classroom and board, and give them a green folder. The green folder includes the $5 booth bucks, a little bit about us, and alternate schedules such as the club schedule. Once we are finished helping them, we have them sign a log that shows that we did that service that day. This log also helps us keep track of our impact on the subs and if we need to change something about it.
